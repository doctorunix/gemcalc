About Me

I am a young and amateur web developer. Started web development back when Web 2.0 was discovering what it was. I never anticipated the web being where it is today, however, I wouldn't take it any other way. The 2-Dimensional flat design is what I've always wanted and never realized it. Gradients, excessive shadows, high contrast colors that made no sense have always been an eyesore. Here you will find doodles and small things I've thrown together. I'm not the most amazing programmer, however, what I do make sure is that the user interface and experience are something that everyone will enjoy and understand I consider myself an advanced if not expert user, and when I come across an application for computer or mobile that is clunky or non-intuitive, I tend to being looking for something better almost immediately.
